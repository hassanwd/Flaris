› AN EXCEPTIONAL ALTERNATIVE ‹

Nowadays, time is extremely valuable. FLARIS LAR 1 goes to war with long and tiring travels and baggage claims. It is an answer to the
challenges of modern transportation. It is an alternative to congested highways and cruise flights.

Independence

You gain a new dimension of freedom by investing in LAR 01. Thanks to its ability to operate on grass airstrips, Flaris allows you to avoid large airports and get closer to your destination point. There are over 10,000 aeroclubs and 45,000 grass airstrips in the world. Flaris needs just 200 m of paved runway to takeoff and 250 m to land. We have 124 airstrips and 62 civilian airports in Poland, according to the Register of Aerodromes and Airstrips (ULC) as of the 5th of April 2019.

The Earth becomes smaller

Such flexibility is not achievable with any other jet aircraft in the world.

Economy

Convenience

LAR 01 meets the co mmunication needs of the business world. It will quickly and comfortably get you to any destination. It is an option for private individuals who value their time and the comfort of travel as well. Taking off from aeroclub airstrips allows you to save the time spent on getting to the airport and customs procedures. You will also land closer to your destination.

› PURCHASE

Purchasing LAR 01 is the most complete and apparent way to own an aircraft. It is especially popular among corporate clientele, service groups and medium-sized companies, and individual clients. In many countries, such investment can be amortised through advantageous tax deductions.

› LEASING

A form of usage attractive to companies that do not want to or are not able to purchase a jet. Leasing allows using the LAR 01 in a periodic payment system. After a fixed period, it is possible to acquire the aircraft and include it into the assets of your company.

› RENTAL

An ideal option for corporate and individual clients who only occasionally use private aircraft transport. It is possible to pilot the jet or use the services of professional pilots, depending on the qualifications of the client. In the latter case, rental resembles an air taxi service.
